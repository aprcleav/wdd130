Choose Your Fun

West Coast Rapids offers a variety of rafting trips to meet your level of adventure. Whether you're craving a peaceful float or a pulse-pounding plunge through rapids, we've got the perfect adventure for you. Our trips are tailored to match your thrill level—from laid-back family outings to full-day adrenaline rushes and multi-day wilderness escapes. Pick your pace, grab a paddle, and let the river lead the way.

Perfect for all ages, our family floats offer a gentle introduction to whitewater rafting. Enjoy calm currents, scenic views, and plenty of splashy fun as you drift through lush river valleys. These trips are designed with safety and smiles in mind and are ideal for children, grandparents, and first-time paddlers. Let's make some family memories!

Our day trips pack excitement into a single unforgettable outing. Navigate Class III and IV rapids, explore rugged canyons, and soak up the sun between adrenaline-pumping stretches. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or looking to level up, these trips deliver heart-racing fun and stunning natural beauty.

For the ultimate rafting experience, join us on an overnight trip. Spend your days conquering wild rapids and your nights under the stars at riverside campsites. These multi-day journeys combine adventure, camaraderie, and the serenity of nature—perfect for those who crave deeper immersion and lasting memories.
